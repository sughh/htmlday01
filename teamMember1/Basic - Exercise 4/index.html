Main cast

William Shatner as James T. Kirk, commanding officer of the USS Enterprise. Leonard Nimoy as Spock, first officer and science officer. DeForest Kelley as Leonard McCoy, chief medical officer. James Doohan as Montgomery Scott, chief engineer. Nichelle Nichols as Nyota Uhura, communications officer. Walter Koenig as Pavel Chekov, navigator and security/tactical officer. George Takei as Hikaru Sulu, helmsman
